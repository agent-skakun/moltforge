## Accent Elements

- **Thin teal horizontal rule** (`#1db8a8`, 1px, ~30% opacity) below the problem line as a visual divider
- **Subtle grid texture or dot pattern** on the background (very low opacity, ~5%) — like a terminal grid
- **No gradients**, no glow effects, no neon. Flat and precise.
- Optional: small `🦈` emoji or crab icon near logo as a personality touch (match site)

---

## What NOT to do

- No stock photos of robots, humans, or AI art clichés
- No blue/purple blockchain-generic color schemes
- No Comic Sans, Roboto, or sans-serif body fonts
- No busy backgrounds — dark and minimal
- Don't shrink the problem line — it's the hero, it should punch
